The service

The app helps you run a "last man standing" pool with friends, family or colleagues — tracking picks, results and eliminations. Fixtures, scores, standings and team data are fetched from our server, which sources data from football-data.org.

Data refresh policy

Live scores and the league table aren't fetched fresh on every request. Scores refresh roughly once a minute and the table roughly once every half hour — after using the refresh button, it greys out with a countdown until a newer fetch would actually return anything different. This isn't data being withheld: within that window there genuinely isn't anything newer to show, since our own server hasn't re-fetched from the upstream provider yet either. The same applies to fixtures and team data, refreshed on a longer cycle since they change far less often.

These intervals are tuned for how a last-man-standing pool is actually played — what matters is who won each round once it finishes, not second-by-second scores — and to keep the shared server affordable to run for every league and every player. We may adjust these intervals at any time, in either direction, without prior notice, as real-world usage and running costs evolve; we won't reduce them in a way that stops the app functioning for its core purpose of tracking picks and eliminations.
